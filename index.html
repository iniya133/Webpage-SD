What is Digitalization?

Digitalization is the integration of digital technologies into everyday life. Such integration is possible by the digitization of information. Digitization is defined as the process of converting physically collected information (e.g., sensors, written information, etc.) and knowledge into a computer-readable language.

Desalination without Electricity

Digital Technology is great but it is not the only technology that will solve all global concerns. To tackle such problems we need scientific technologies as well. Today, there are a number of desalination plants across the globe, but it is estimated that approximately 1.5 KWh is required for 1 cubic meter. Also, The brine solution that is dumped back into the sea can cause danger to the aquatic ecosystem. A study shows that 67 tons of CO2 is produced during desalination for 1000 cubic meters of sea water.

The solution to this problem is Solar Desalination.
